# Corporate Contract Policy Rules (Mock)

1. **Indemnity clauses**: Any clause requiring our company to indemnify the counterparty for
   unlimited liability must be flagged. Capped indemnity (with a stated maximum) is acceptable.
2. **Automatic renewal**: Any clause that auto-renews the contract without an opt-out window
   of at least 30 days must be flagged.
3. **Unlimited liability**: Any clause removing liability caps entirely must be flagged.
4. **Payment terms**: Payment terms longer than 90 days must be flagged for finance review.
5. **Governing law**: Contracts not governed by a mutually agreed jurisdiction must be flagged.
